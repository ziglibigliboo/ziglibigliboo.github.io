The Story

Wokman is a culinary festival and every stakeholder, be it the customer, the franchisee, or the wizard behind the counter, all are active participants in this celebration of ‘healthy fast food!’ Wokman is a unique culinary experience for the mobile and the upwardly mobile. A refined palate seeks good, freshly cooked food even in a shopping mall or the airport waiting for lounge.

The hustle can’t be interrupted because time is of essence and extravagance is not the norm of the day. It’s quick, healthy, wholesome, nutritious, and easy on both the pocket and the belly! To top it all, the customer directs the show, selecting the meats, vegetables, sauces (the in-house secret magic potions developed over the years), and accompaniments. Vegan-not forget the vegans and meat lovers have ample choice when they stir up their customized bowls. Wokman encourages customers to bring out the chef in them.
